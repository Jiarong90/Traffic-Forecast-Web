Project introduction

Forecasting Analytics for Singapore Traffic--FAST is a Singapore traffic forecasting and monitoring demo platform designed to integrate accident visibility, live camera evidence, route planning, weather information, alerts, and user feedback into one system.

The project aims to present traffic information in a clearer and more practical way for both public users and operational-side users, so that they can understand road conditions faster, compare route options more effectively, and respond to disruptions with better situational awareness.

Vision and mission

Vision & Mission

-Help people better understand traffic conditions and make better travel decisions.

-Instead of only showing current traffic information, the system focuses on predicting future traffic impacts.

-Allows users to plan their trips earlier and avoid possible congestion.
